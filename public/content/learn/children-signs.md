# Signs of Sleep Disordered Breathing in Children

- Clenching/Grinding
- Excessive Napping
- Bed wetting
- ADHD symptoms and irritability
- Sleep walking
- Mouth Breathing
- Sweating
- Lots of Movement
- Restless sleep and frequent waking
- Very Light or Deep Sleep
- Snoring
- Fatigue or Brain Fog
- Growth problems
- Pauses in Breathing
