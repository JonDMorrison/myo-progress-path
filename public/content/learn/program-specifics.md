# Myofunctional Therapy Program Specifics

Each set of exercises will typically follow a similar format: each day there will be **three active exercises**, **one passive exercise** and **one breathing or posture exercise** unless otherwise noted. Some weeks may vary slightly from this format, such as pre and post frenectomy weeks.

## Exercise Types

**Active exercises** require a **mirror** to ensure that exercises are being completed as instructed while minimizing **compensations** (more information on compensations later).

**Passive exercises** require less focus and can be done during another activity.

**Breathing and Posture exercises** help develop the necessary nasal breathing and postural habits needed.

## Supporting Materials

**Videos and/or photos** will be provided where applicable to help teach each exercise. There will be **check-ins** included to help develop an awareness surrounding the percentage of time spent nasal breathing and percentage of time the tongue is on the correct spot.
