# Introduction to Myofunctional Therapy

Myofunctional Therapy is a similar concept to physiotherapy but focuses on the muscles of the face and tongue. It is used to fix incorrect oral functions related to breathing, swallowing, and chewing that may have developed as early as infancy. Participating in this program will allow for improvements in function, behaviours, and the healthy structural development of the face and airway in children. This is accomplished through daily exercises and stretches that will be taught in this program. Myofunctional therapy does have its limitations and may need to be corrected alongside other specialists.
